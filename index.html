ABOUT

Monart City is an impact oriented project developed by a holding company in formation in the State of Wyoming under the name MONART INDUSTRIES DAO,LLC.

It that owns operating companies carrying out the organizational operating process to deliver partnerships ,tools,products that positions the project to be successful.The group consists of family offices,corporate bodies,information technology companies,investment firms ,real estate management organizations as well as visionaries,artists,architects,designers,scientists and other key industrial role individuals,who are collaborating to design and fabricate a framework for planetary systems,through innovations in sustainable architecture,technology and Infrastructure design.

We take advantage of the principles of nature’s own patterned systems,to develop an ecosystem built on zero emissions.

VISION

Monart City plans to have its foundations created on efficient energy production ,Data curation and open science research ,creating a smooth transition for the development of the Mega City therefore in the first phases of development we plan to build a distributed energy pipeline of renewable energy power plants equipped with efficient mini grids collectively capable of generating nominal power output of about 150MW a year .Whiles in the process setting up light industrial facilities equipped with efficient technology that can harness the power produced and put it into productive use.

We believe that building an interdependent integrated system between the entire development’s core infrastructures which is Real Estate,Distributed Energy and P2P Data Communication systems for research,innovation and inventory will set the project on a successful path.And in regards to future development of Monart City and other areas of applications where this new research findings can deployed,data from the our initial phases of development is detrimental in the emergence of a fully autonomous,decentralized Monart City.
